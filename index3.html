Our Services

What we do best.

Incidence Responding Services

Consult with one of our certified forensic before you make a decision on any civil or criminal matter. Our 0-Day response team can perform a complete forensics analysis as quickly as 72 hours and will perform on-site volatile data analysis with little or no disruption to any system

Electronic Discovery

We offer eDiscovery solutions to address the evolving Federal Regulations and Compliance issues surrounding Electronic Document Management or Electronically Stored Information.

Computer Forensics Preliminary Analysis

Frequested, a preliminary analysis can be conducted at a fraction of the cost in order to establish evidence or lack thereof. The drawback may be missed evidence located in free drive space or encrypted files and containers.

Data Recovery

We are experts in recovering failed RAIDs; Recovering lost partitions, passwords, and files are routine procedures for our experts who have a perfect record to date. We utilize the latest Clean Room Class 100 technology and the very latest computer data recovery techniques.
